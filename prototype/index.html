Top View

This view displays your personal top 4 most listened tracks for one month (short term), six months (medium term) and of all time you use Spotify for (long term). Below you can find a graph that displays various audio features based on your average 50 most listened songs for the different periods. If you click on any cover image of a song, you will be led to that song's more detailed information page. If you click on one of the periods, you will find an overview of your 50 most listened songs for that period.
